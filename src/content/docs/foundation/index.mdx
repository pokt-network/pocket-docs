The Pocket Network Foundation (PNF) is the non-profit steward of Pocket Network. PNF exists to grow, sustain, and protect the decentralized infrastructure that powers Pocket — not to extract value from it.

## What PNF Does

**Infrastructure operations.** PNF operates the public API portal at [api.pocket.network](https://api.pocket.network), providing free RPC access to 60+ blockchains with no API key required. This portal serves as the default entry point for developers building on Pocket.

**Ecosystem growth.** PNF pursues partnerships with chain foundations, exchanges, and ecosystem builders. We handle business development, co-marketing, and integration support for teams building on or with Pocket Network.

**Protocol stewardship.** PNF contributes to protocol development, funds public goods through the DAO treasury, and coordinates governance processes. We don't control the protocol — we serve it.

**Community support.** PNF maintains documentation, community channels, and developer resources. We're the team behind these docs, the [Pocket Discord](http://discord.gg/pocket-network), and ecosystem tooling.

## Sustainability Model

PNF operates as a non-profit. We don't take profit margins on relay traffic or charge rent-seeking fees. Our sustainability comes from the DAO treasury allocation (10% of minted POKT), grants, and partnerships. This non-profit structure means the network's costs stay dramatically lower than centralized alternatives.
